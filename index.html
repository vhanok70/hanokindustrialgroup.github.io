Aluminium Ingots A7 (99.7%)

Aluminium Ingots A7 are high‑purity products containing at least 99.7% aluminium, classified as primary aluminium and known for excellent corrosion resistance and electrical conductivity. [web:1][web:14]

  • Purity: 99.7% minimum Al content
  • Typical weight: 20–25 kg per ingot, strapped in bundles of ~1–1.2 MT
  • Applications: Automotive castings, electrical components, building profiles, packaging and general casting. [web:4]
  • Advantages: Low melting point, good fluidity, high conductivity and consistent quality for re‑melting. [web:1][web:4]

Hanok Industrial Group focuses on stable supply programs, offering containerized shipments with standard documentation and third‑party inspection when required.

Copper Cathodes

Copper cathodes are high‑purity rectangular plates of refined copper, typically reaching 99.97–99.99% purity through electrolytic refining. [web:6][web:12]

  • Purity: Up to 99.99% Cu, often certified as LME Grade A or ASTM B115. [web:12][web:15]
  • Dimensions: Commonly around 0.5–1 m² surface area and 3–20 mm thickness, depending on producer. [web:6][web:18]
  • Applications: Wire rod, cables, tubes, sheets and over 400 copper‑based alloys in construction and energy sectors. [web:6][web:15]
  • Benefits: Excellent electrical and thermal conductivity, traceable production, and consistent performance in downstream processes. [web:6][web:12]

Typical shipments are palletized cathode bundles suitable for container loading, with assay certificates and packing lists attached.

Non‑Ferrous Scrap

Non‑ferrous scrap such as aluminium and copper scrap is a critical raw material for secondary metal production and supports circular economy in metal industries. [web:13][web:10]

  • Grades: Selected aluminium scrap, copper scrap and mixed non‑ferrous grades based on buyer specification.
  • Usage: Re‑melting in foundries, rolling mills and secondary smelters to produce new ingots, billets or castings. [web:10][web:19]
  • Advantages: Reduced energy consumption and lower environmental impact compared to primary metal production. [web:10]

Hanok Industrial Group focuses on clean grades and transparent inspection to minimize quality disputes and maximize recovery for buyers.
